Our History
Hot Beans Web was founded in 2013 by our CEO Jamie Dornan. He wanted to share his passion for web development, he decided to create Hot Beans Web as he couldn't find anywhere or anyone who could create his ideal website. He originally opened the firm in Armagh, Northern Ireland but due to rapid growth we are now located in Belfast, NI, we have also expanded to Canberra, Australia.
